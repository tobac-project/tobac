# User Guide


*tobac*, as with much scientific software, has a number of different options, parameters, and ways of analyzing the resulting output. The goal of this guide is to show the different ways of applying *tobac* and help you make the best choices for your science questions.

## Collaborate and get Help

The *tobac* team is always happy to collaborate with you and make sure your tracking is completed as appropriately for your problem as possible. If you are interested in collaborating, feel free to e-mail any of the [lead developers](/userguide/governance).
